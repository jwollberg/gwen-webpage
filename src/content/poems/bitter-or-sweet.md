carry me through lacey ribbons
turn me into straw
look into my eyes to see what i have seen
the flames shoot out of my mouth
then melt into ocean waters
beautiful waves splash into the sunlight
the sunlight of blisters and burns
melting down
into paths of long lacey ribbons
