shadows are dancing between my eyes, as 
I’m gliding around the world trying to get in
my tears, once burning, now turn to ice
frost biting my face, as I notice your cloudy whispers
your colors seem so jagged, yet, 
I want to be part of your circle of pigeons
I’m falling into this ravine 
someone, please open a door

the sparkles in my eyes seem frail
tattered and torn and ripped at the seams
useless,
             nameless,
                             faceless,
                                            and lonely...
and crying
and crying
and crying
and drowning
