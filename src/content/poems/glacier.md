sail this frostbitten boat
to a different shore
i tune my cracked guitar
can’t take it anymore

you’re stacking shards of ice
In your bottomless hearts
and taking pictures of skies
that are breaking apart

you’ve shaped an icy world
of tinsel, glass, and foil,
aluminum forests,
and cardboard scraps for soil

burn out the northern lights
count up every mistake
don’t know how many times
you’ve all made my heart break

you’ve quickened the melting
of lost ice, far from sand
i’m alone on this boat
and i don’t understand
