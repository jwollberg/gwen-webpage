distant heartaches seem to 
flutter past now 
once engraved in the ruins of 
one-sided love
now able to embrace
new butterflies
flying along my spine and 
all throughout my soul
a herd of wonderful thoughts
gallop through my mind
as I take in your internal beauty
new love
washes away the remains of the 
bitter bleakness
birthing meaning
to an empty, loveless life
