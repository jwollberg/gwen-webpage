if I were a flower
I’d only be a weed
a dying dandelion
with ants all over me

if I were a TV
black and white I’d be
sitting in the closet
with dust all over me

if I were a letter
I’d only be a ‘z’
ignored by everyone
useless I would be

if everyone was salt 
and I was just one grain
it really wouldn’t mater
if you took that grain away

if I were a person
I’d only just be me
not meaning one damn thing
to anyone I see
