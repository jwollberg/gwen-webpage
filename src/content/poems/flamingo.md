there was a little flame 
inside this little heart 
i recall very well 
it could never burn out 

it only burned for him 
it burned so very high 
but then he went away 
and then that flame did die 

i dwell on how it felt 
a love that seemed unreal
but then he passed away 
now, pain is all i feel 

i’m sorry it was you 
what happened was not fair 
you’re so special to me 
the love we shared was rare 

i will remember you 
this flame will never cease 
i’ll never be with you 
but you are here with me 

dedicated to robbie
