i sometimes wonder what it would be like
if there wasn’t pain, no suffering in sight

i sometimes wonder how it would be
if sickness and sorrow ceased to be

if there were no hate or tears
no sins or fears
no homeless or starving
no drugs or dying
the world would be a better place

i sometimes wonder what life could be
if we all felt genuine empathy

i sometimes wonder how it would feel
if love were pure, if kindness were real

if there were no guns or knives
no stolen lives
no killing in wars
no one closing doors
the world would be a better place

do you ever wonder what it would be like
If we could all live a peaceful life?
