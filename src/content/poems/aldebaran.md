sometimes
when the water is warm
and the heavens can be seen,
and the nighttime sky is upon me…
i can see another dimension,
another world,
a peaceful place
where faeries go.
a secret life,
beyond your dreams,
with crystal-like palaces
and beautiful scenes.
with unicorns grazing
and galloping too,
across sparkling waters
in the eye of the bull…

a following taurus girl,
not mystified,
by her surroundings
nor her life.
she plays the harp
of enchanted dreams
and hopes someday
that she can be
on aldebaran.
