why have you erased me from your mind
i know what you think it’s not of mankind

killing children for their faces is 
so much fun for you
you try to hide behind these masks
because all you have is your feeble mind

you fly away and crash your plane
but it’s not too much for you

you fall down a mountain and 
crack open your head
while i’m at home 
painting the walls black
