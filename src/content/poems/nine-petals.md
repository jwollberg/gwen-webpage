separating fine lines of blackness
hiding in the light
are you there below, in the darkness
or are you above with the creator
are you watching
are you crying
are you missing anything
you escaped from your cage
are you in a new one
or are you free now
lost from the world
somewhere in sleep
still on my mind
still in my heart
nine flower petals fall from the stem that
sleeps above you
the sun is long burned out
done splintering your eyes
now another can see
not me...still foggy on this mountain
are you flying
are you falling
one day we will meet again
maybe in heaven
maybe somewhere else

dedicated to Robbie
