my lungs collapse

yet i’m still inhaling

blue plastic wrap

i shine my candle

into your wide-open ears

i sing to your face

as i cover it with black coal

alone, i draw a pentacle

on my birthday,

blow out my pretend candles,

as i wish to be dead

because i’m still inhaling

your blue plastic wrap
