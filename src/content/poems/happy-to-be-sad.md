i’d be happy to be

someone else

i hate the things i see—

you’re behind me now and

i can’t look back

the fog is too thick

i cannot see

my eyes are falling out
 
happy…

             …sad
 
happy to be sad

the clouds crash together

the sun hides in fear

i’m sinking in a river that the

clouds have helped me cry
 
yet i am happy…

happy to be sad
