beautiful thoughts      

of what could be        

forget the world         

your mind is free       
 
there is a place           

where minds run to    

when all the world     

is falling through       
 
the sweetness shines  

past all the hate          

it gives you peace      

it blocks the pain       
 
there is no death         

there is no hate           

all the sadness            

will fade away           
 
are there such things  

as peace and love?     

can the whole world  

unite as one?              
 
all that is real             

is what we see            

peace _is_ unreal           

it’s just a dream
