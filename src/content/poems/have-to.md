i am so sweet and innocent,

i would never lie.

i am so mature & responsible,

i do not fuss or cry.
 
i am a thousand sunrays,

on a cloudy day.

I’m a freshly opened blossom,

on the first day of spring.
 
i would never do anything

displeasing or wrong.

i will always be perfect for you,

i will never let you down.
 
but you hate me
 
i hate you for everything,

you’ve made my life a lie.

now i’m out to kill you,

no, i’m not afraid to try.
 
now i am a little devil,

just here to make you mad.

you cannot hurt my feelings,

you cannot make me sad.
 
now i’m a thunderstorm,

on your perfect days.

i’m a wilting black rose

that refuses to stay.
 
i am a black crow,

in your cornfield,

i am the maggots,

that eat your rotting corpse as a meal.
 
i don’t have to do anything anymore,

’cause you were not truthful.

you said you’d love me until you died…

but you’re still alive.
