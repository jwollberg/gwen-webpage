death is here
as the sadness creeps across my face
my time is over
i’m drowning
sinking in a deep depression
death rises as i fall
my wrists are stained
my eyes are overflowing
i love…i hate
i wish it would end
please come back and rescue me
i’m falling,
tumbling down this black hole
a volcano…hell
i only wanted to swim free
with you here right beside me
i’m calling,
now i’m screaming loudly
the black shadows return my calls
the angels are singing off-key
their harps are broken
their eyes rolled back
demons are chanting, “come to us”
all i wanted was for you to love me
now i’m broken…i’m in pieces
drowning in a river of tears and blood
i’m burning and loving
crying and bleeding
screaming…losing love
losing life
missing, wishing, killing us both
