the seeds are planted
one by one they grow
into the garden
she neglects
beautiful flower garden
she'll tend to it later

people come and go
admiring her garden
she assumes it's beautiful
but she'll get to it later

time goes on in her world of 
cross-words
she gets up from her chair
and glances out the window
her garden is wilting and
overgrown with weeds

she sits back down
and continues to rock with 
meaningless words
