today is such a lonely day
i only think of you
but now you’re gone forever
and i don’t know what to do

the sky was so much prettier
when you were here with me
but now i’m all alone
and rain is all i see

i call your name; there’s no reply
no hand to hold, no arms stretched wide
the world moves on, yet here i lie
lost without you by my side

dedicated to Robbie
