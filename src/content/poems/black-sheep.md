i’m getting tired of 
stumbling over dead bodies,
eating toothpaste, and 
watching the trees set fire

i hear all the blind robots
leak out their paint

the way you kill pigeons
makes me wonder if your diamonds melt

you’ve made the moon angry
the sun hangs low with depression 
the clouds above you have a green swirl

the way you burn the legs off spiders, and 
mow away the flowers, and 
breed black kats, and 
bite your nails--
as you flock over your dead sheep--
makes me realize you have no sympathy

i’m getting tired of 
slipping in blue paint
i’m getting sick of 
stumbling over dead bodies
