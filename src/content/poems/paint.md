the plastic paint

you spit at me is blue

you turn me blue

plastic face

plastic heart

plastic soul

plastic smile

nothing about you is real

it’s all fake

you’re just a copy

a clone of robots

and all you do is stumble

as you leak out your paint

blue paint

i’m drenched in blue
